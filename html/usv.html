Unmanned Surfave Vehicle (USV) for Water Sampling

This project was created as my bachelor's thesis. The goal of this USV is to sample water at a certain depth automatically. This project is divided into 2 parts, namely USV and water sampler.

The USV is a catamaran and uses PixHawk Pix32 for the main controller, GPS module for navigation, 1 Brushless DC Motor, and 1 Servo motor as the actuator. The water sampler system can work semi-separately from the USV, this system is controlled using Arduino Nano and communicates with Pixhawk via the PWM signal from the Pixhawk to determine the depth of water intake. This system is designed to take water samples with a certain volume (220 ml per bottle) to ensure accuracy and ease of operation in various water conditions.

Skills: C++, PixHawk, Arduino
