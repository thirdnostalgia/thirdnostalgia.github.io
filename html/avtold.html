AVTOLD

This project involves creating a drone for the Kontes Robot Terbang Indonesia (KRTI) competition in the Vertical Takeoff and Landing (VTOL) division. The mission for this division requires each team to operate their drones fully autonomously in an area representing a logistics delivery zone with specific QR code locations designated for package delivery.

As the electrical engineer for this project, my contributions included designing and assembling the electronic components necessary for the drone's operation, ensuring reliable power distribution and component integration. This drone uses Pixhawk flight controller as its main brain which is control components like motors, sensors, and communication modules and also uses Raspberry Pi for the computer vision task.



Skills: Electronics, Drone

Contribution:

  • Choosing and make the electronic schematic for the robot's operation.
  • Soldering and wiring the electronic components
  • Ensuring all electronic components work seamlessly together, from the power supply to the sensors and actuators.
